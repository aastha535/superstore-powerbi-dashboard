Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Superstore sales data and uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and shipping trends. The dashboard is designed to support business decision-making through clear KPIs and visual storytelling.

Objectives

Analyze overall sales, profit, and order volume

Track year-over-year (YoY) sales and profit trends

Understand performance by category and sub-category

Compare sales across regions and cities

Study payment modes and shipping methods

Tools Used

Power BI

CSV dataset (Superstore data)

Key Insights

Sales and profit show noticeable variation across months and years

Technology and Office Supplies contribute significantly to total sales

Certain cities and regions consistently outperform others

Standard Class is the most used shipping mode

Cash on Delivery (COD) and online payments form a major share of transactions

Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how Power BI can be used to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through effective visualization and KPI tracking.
